Anonymous Chat Box

One of the key features of our platform is an anonymous chat system where junior students can ask questions without revealing their identity. Random seniors from the platform respond, ensuring unbiased and open communication. This system helps build trust and encourages even shy students to seek guidance freely.

Announcement Section

Our announcement board keeps you updated on both internal and external opportunities. From college fest schedules to outside hackathons, you'll find everything here. Seniors can also post if they are looking for team members or want to share useful opportunities with juniors. Each announcement comes with links for quick registration and more details.

Experience Wall

Seniors from various domains share their personal experiences, including internships, campus placements, and project journeys. This wall serves as a real-time learning feed for juniors, inspiring them and providing practical advice on what to expect and how to prepare.

Our Vision

We envision a college ecosystem where mentorship and collaboration are natural parts of student life. With interactive tools and a secure space, we aim to make sure no student feels left behind in their academic or career journey.
